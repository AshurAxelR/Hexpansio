Income and Resources

  • Happiness

    increases city Happiness. Unhappy cities cannot function efficiently and can be consumed by The Void.

  • Food

    increases population in the city.

  • Gold

    is required for expanding city borders and discovering new lands. ⬤ Gold is shared between cities.

  • Production

    is required for building tile improvements and upgrades.
    50% of excess production is converted to ⬤ Goods, which can be used to hurry production in other cities.
